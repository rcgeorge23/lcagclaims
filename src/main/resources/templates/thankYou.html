What happens next?

A member of our team will process your application. If you provided copies of your ID and scheme documentation we will use these to verify your identity. Once we have completed our checks we will delete your documents and get back to you via email. If you are an existing Big Group member and provided your Big Group username, we will contact you via the Big Group forum in order to confirm your identity.


Full membership and LCAG Funding

In order to become a full LCAG member, we ask for a small contribution towards our fighting fund.

The majority of the work we do is free of charge, the website and forum and other materials are supplied and supported by members in their spare time. However some of our activities do require funding. For example, PR, consultancy and advertising.

Most of our members have each contributed £100 to the fund. We understand that peoples’ circumstances are very different and we will accept a smaller amount if you are experiencing financial difficulties. We also understand that people are sometimes skeptical about generic funding requests. To address this, as part of our regular update email (usually every 2 - 3 weeks), we provide a breakdown of our income and expenditure along with the groups current bank account balance. Any member can request to see a full bank statement at any time should they wish to do so.
